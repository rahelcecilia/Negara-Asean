Laksa Singapore

Laksa Singapore is a noodle dish with coconut milk gravy rich in Malay spices. It is usually served with shrimp, pieces of tofu pong, fish cake, lime slices and sambal belacan.

Ingredients :
- vermicelli
- Shrimp
- Filleted Chicken Breast
- Tofu, Sprout
- Lemongrass Stems, Lime Leaves, Galangal
- Galangal, Coconut Milk, Oil
- Salt, Sugar, Red Chili
- Dried Shrimp, Ginger, Red Onion, Garlic
- Shrimp Paste, Roasted, Turmeric, Coriander
- Red Chili, Lime, Basil

Hokkien Prawn Mee Noodle

Hokkien Prawn Mee which is very famous and loved by Singaporeans. This food is a mixture of yellow noodles and vermicelli noodles which are dipped in broth, then boiled with seafood.

Ingredients :
- Dry Hokkien Noodles
- Green Scallop Meat
- Egg, Shrimp Broth, Shrimp
- Vegetable Oil
-Garlic
- Oyster Sauce, Soy Sauce, Lime
- Salt, Pepper Powder, Chives, Sprouts

Singapore Sauce Crab

Popular crab preparations from Singapore which are cooked in a sweet and savory sauce with a slightly spicy touch.

Ingredients :
- Crab
- Lemongrass Stems, Bay Leaves, Orange Leaves
- Egg, Chili Powder, Chili & Tomato Sauce
- Plum Sauce, Salt, Sugar, Flavoring, Water
- Scallions, Shallots & Garlic
- Ginger, Tomato, Red Chili

Hainanese Chicken Rice

A popular dish in Singapore, Malaysia and Thailand, this juicy and succulent steamed chicken is always a feast!

Ingredients :
- Chicken
- Salt, Ginger, Garlic, Shallot
- Scallions, Sesame Oil, Salt
- Sugar, Pepper
- Fluffy Rice
- Chicken Broth, Sweet Soy Sauce
- Margarine
- Sambal Sauce, Sirracha Sauce, Lime
- Broth
- Cucumber, Tomato, Cilantro

Fish Head Curry

Singapore Fish Head Curry is a culinary specialty that combines three ethnicities, India, China and Asia. Snapper fish head mixed with red sauce and thick curry sauce full of spices.

Ingredients :
- Head of Red Snapper
- Okra Fruit
- Eggplant,Tomato
- Water, Coconut Milk
- Lemongrass Stems, Oil, Coriander
- Fish Sauce, Salt, Sugar, Broth Powder
- Shallots, Garlic
- Galangal, Ginger, Candlenut, Shrimp Paste, Chilli Paste
- Turmeric, Cumin, Tamarind
