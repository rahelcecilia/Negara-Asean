Lumpia

Lumpia consists of a thin sheet of wheat flour which is used as a wrapper for the filling which is generally bamboo shoots, eggs, fresh vegetables, meat or seafood. Lumpia is a typical food from Semarang, Jawa Tengah.

Ingredients :
- Bamboo shoot
- Chicken
- Garlic and Leek
- Cooking Oil
- Shrimp
- Eggs
- Chicken Broth
- Brown Sugar, Salt, Sugar
- Fish Sauce, Oyster Sauce
- Mushroom flavoring

Ayam Betutu

Betutu is a type of traditional Balinese food whose raw materials are whole duck and chicken carcasses.

Ingredients :
- Chicken
- Salt, Turmeric, Ginger
- Lemongrass Stems, Cassava Leaves, Banana Leaves, Orange Leaves
- Lime Water, Flavor
- Oil
- Onion, Garlic, Red Chili, Coriander, Galangal

Soto Banjar

Soto Banjar is a typical soto of the Banjar tribe, South Kalimantan, with the main ingredient being chicken and fragrant spices such as cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves.

Ingredients :
- Chicken
- Water
- Vermicelli
- Shallot, Garlic, Ginger
- Nutmeg, Pepper, Cinnamon, Cloves, Cardamom
- Lawang Flowers, Celery Leaves, Leek, Onions
- Eggs, Potatoes
- Salt, Sugar
- Flavor
- Margarine & Cooking Oil

Palumara

Palumara fish in spicy and sour sauce, a typical food from Palu and Makassar, on the island of Sulawesi.

Ingredients :
- Milkfish
- Lemongrass, Tamarind
- Water
- Garlic, Red Chili, Tomato, Shallot
- Flavor
- Brown Sugar, Orange Leaves, Oil
- White Pepper, Turmeric, Coriander, Basil

Papeda

Papeda is food in the form of sago porridge typical of Maluku and Papua which is usually served with tuna or mubara fish seasoned with turmeric.

Ingredients :
- Sago Flour
- Chicken Broth
- Garlic
- Salt
- Water
