Tom Yum Goong

Tom Yam Goong is a soup that originates from Thailand. In Thailand, tom yum goong is usually made with mixed shrimp, chicken, fish or seafood and mushrooms.

Ingredients :
- Shrimp
- Squid
- Meatball
- Water
- Chicory
- Enoki Mushroom
- Carrot
- Bay Leaves, Lemongrass Stems, Galangal, Lime Leaves
- Tomato, Cilantro, Lime
- Salt, Sugar, Pepper, Mushroom Broth
- Shallots, Garlic, Pecan
- Red Chili, Ginger, Baked Shrimp

Gai Med Ma Moung

Stir-fried cashew nuts is one of the favorite menus for Thai food lovers.

Ingredients :
- Chicken Breast
- Salt, Cooking Oil
- Thai Chilies
- Cashews
- Small Onion, Cabbage
- Garlic, Green Onions
- Oyster Sauce, Soy Sauce, Black Soy Sauce

Kaeng Khiao Wan

Kaeng khiao wan or green curry is a kind of green curry dish originating from Central Thailand.

Ingredients :
- Chicken
- Green Eggplant, Green Tomato
- Coconut Milk, Broth Water
- Orange Leaves, Basil Leaves, Lemongrass
- Sugar, Salt, Oil
- Green Chilli, Cayenne Pepper
- Roasted Cumin
- Shallots, Garlic
- Pepper Powder, Coriander Powder

Sweet Cassava

Thai cassava which is a typical Thai food which is suitable for dessert.

Ingredients :
- Cassava
- Sugar
- Water
- Salt
- Thick Coconut Milk
- Pandan Leaves
- Cornstarch

Kari Massaman

Massaman curry is a type of Thai food that has been known since the 17th century. This delicious food is usually served with beef or chicken pieces.

Ingredients :
- Chicken
- Carrots, Potatoes, Tomatoes
- Brown Sugar, Onion, Red Chili, Garlic
- Cardamom, Lawang Flowers, Bay Leaves, Lime Leaves, Tamarind
- Coconut Milk, Salt, Water
- Turmeric, Pepper
- Dried Chili, Cayenne Pepper, Galangal
- Lemongrass, Coriander, Cumin
- Cinnamon, Cooking Oil
