Home of the Green Revolution

Green Revolution is the growth or increase in food production, especially grains, wheat and rice, which are introduced through new varieties.

Chicken Adobo

Chicken adobo is made from chicken (can also be pork or seafood) cooked in soy sauce, vinegar and garlic.

Ingredients :
- Chicken
- Garlic, Bay Leaves, Onion
- Soy Sauce, Vinegar, Sweet Soy Sauce
- Sugar, Pepper, Paprika, Oil

Buko Pie

Buko in Tagalog means coconut. In other words, buko pie is a pie made from coconut fruit. This snack is a mandatory snack for the people of the Philippines.

Ingredients :
- Wheat Flour, Cornstarch
- Young Coconut Meat
- Margarine, Multipurpose Cream
-Lemon Juice
- Salt, Sugar, Liquid Milk, Egg

Lechon

Lechon or roast pork is a food that must be served at every celebration in the Philippines. Such as birthdays, Christmas, fiesta, and weddings.

Ingredients :
- Pig
- Salt, Black Pepper, Soy Sauce
-Sprites
- Lemongrass, Star Anise, Bay Leaf
- Garlic, Green Onion
- Bananas

Beef Caldereta

Beef Caldereta is a dish made from tomatoes, vinegar paste and pepper. Potatoes and carrots can also be added to make the dish more nutritious.

Ingredients :
- Beef
- Beef Broth
- Liver
- Garlic, Onion
- Red Bell pepper, Green Bell Pepper
- Tomato Sauce
- Chili Flakes, Bay Leaves
- Potatoes, Carrots
- Cooking Oil, Green Olives
- Salt and Pepper

Chicken inasal

This food can be found in almost all corners of Metro Manila. It can also be called grilled or roasted chicken, but this is not just ordinary grilled chicken. Chicken meat is marinated in a mixture of lemongrass, calamansi, salt, pepper and garlic, then brushed with achiete oil.

Ingredients :
- Chicken
- Ginger, Garlic Lemongrass
- Coconut Vinegar, Lemon or Calamansi Juice
- Salt, Brown Sugar
- Lemon Soda, Ground Black Pepper
- Annatto Oil, Margarine
