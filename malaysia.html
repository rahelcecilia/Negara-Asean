Nasi Kandar

Nasi Kandar is the most favorite food in Malaysia. The origin of Kandar rice is from Pulau Pinang, Malaysia.

Ingredients :
- Curry
- Chicken
- Shallots, Garlic
- Sweet Soy Sauce, Salty Soy Sauce, Water, Salt
- Potatoes, Onions, Oyster Sauce
- Green Chillies, Green Tomatoes, Oil

Ayam Percik

Ayam percik is a chicken-based dish typical of the Kelantan region, Malaysia.

Ingredients :
- Chicken
- Lemongrass, Coconut Milk, Broth
- Oil
- Red Chilli, Cayenne Pepper
- Shallots, Garlic
- Coriander, Ginger, Turmeric, Candlenut, Salt

Nasi Kerabu

Nasi kerabu is a Malaysian rice dish, a type of nasi ulam, in which blue rice is eaten with dried fish or fried chicken, crackers, pickles and other salads.

Ingredients :
- Rice
- Beef
- Chicken Broth
- Mackerel
- Salt, Granulated Sugar
- Butterfly Flower
- Lemongrass Stems, Turmeric, Vegetable Oil, Grilled Shrimp Paste
- Red Chili, Tamarind, Lime, Shallot
- White Pepper Powder, Roasted Ginger, Coconut
- Cucumber, Sprouts, White Cabbage, Salted Egg, Crackers

Mee Siam

Mee siam is a dish of thin rice vermicelli of hot, sweet and sour flavours, originated in Penang.

Ingredients :
- Dried Chilies
- Large Onion, Garlic
- Spice Paste
- Rice Vermicelli
- Salted Soybeans
- Fresh Shrimps
- Beansprouts
- Lemongrass, Galangal, Roasted Peanuts
-Tamarind Paste
- Salt to taste, Sugar
- Eggs, Tofu, Chinese Chives, Calamansi Limes

Char Kway Teow

Char Kway Teow is a popular street food in Malaysia. Pre-soaked rice noodles are quickly stir-fried over high heat in single serving batches to get a good charr on the rice noodles.

Ingredients :
- Water
- Flat Wide Rice Noodles
- Red Chillies
- Shallots, Garlic, Lime
- Sugar
- Fish Sauce, Soy Sauce, Dark Soy Caramel
- Ground White Pepper, Peanut Oil
- Raw Shrimp
- Egg, Crunchy Bean Sprouts, Green Onion
