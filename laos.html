Larb Moo

Larb is a Lao meat salad. “Moo” means pork, so Larb Moo is a pork salad. It is regarded as the unofficial national dish of Laos.

Ingredients :
- Ground Pork
- Water
- Toasted Rice Powder
- Ground Chili Powder
- Fish Sauce
- Lime Juice
- Shallots
- Cilantro
- Green Onion
Thai Chili
