The largest oil producer in Southeast Asia. Since the late 20th century, oil and natural gas production have been the most important supports of Brunei's economy, apart from the industrial sector.

Ambuyat

National dish in Brunei that comes from the inner stem of the sago tree

Ingredients :
- Fine Sago / Ambulung
- Dried White Mango
- Fermented Durian
- Cayenne Pepper
- Black Shrimp Paste
- Musk Lime
- Salt and Sugar
